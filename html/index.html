PRIMARY TET

....

The TET department will provide huge examination to the candidates who are applied for the recruitment. Thus the candidates have to prepare well for the examination to score high marks. The candidates will get the admit card to appear for the WB Upper Primary TET examination.

RAILWAY EXAM

....

It includes the following position-Clerks, Goods Guards, Accountants, Traffic Assistants, Commercial-cum-train clerk and the post of Station Master. The exam comprises of four stages - Stage 1 -CBT, Stage 2- CBT, Stage 3- skill test and stage 4 -Document Verification (DV).

WBPSC

.....

The West Bengal Public Service Commission (WBPSC) directs the state Civil Services Exam named WBCS Exam. The exam happens yearly or as and when the Commission declares the date. The WBPSC exam is a three-level assessment Preliminary, Mains, and Interview.
